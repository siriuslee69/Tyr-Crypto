# Asymmetric Verification Reference Licenses

Date: 2026-07-12

This summary is for repository maintenance and is not legal advice.

## NIST Technical Series

The unmodified FIPS PDFs are republished under the NIST Technical Series terms
described at <https://www.nist.gov/open/license>. NIST grants a worldwide right
to reprint Technical Series works and requests the citation followed by:
“Republished courtesy of the National Institute of Standards and Technology.”

## RFC Documents

The unmodified RFC text files retain their complete authorship, copyright, and
license notices. IETF Trust Legal Provisions section 3(c)(i) permits full,
unmodified redistribution outside the IETF standards process:
<https://trustee.ietf.org/documents/trust-legal-provisions/tlp-5/>.

## IACR ePrint

*Taming the many EdDSAs* is distributed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
as stated by its ePrint metadata: <https://eprint.iacr.org/2020/1244>. The
publisher rejected the automated PDF request during this audit, so the lock
records an upstream-only source and no local or partial copy is tracked.

## Candidate Specifications

No clear redistribution grant was found for the candidate specifications used
by Kyber, SPHINCS+, Falcon, FrodoKEM, BIKE, Classic McEliece, NTRU, or SABER.
They are therefore not newly tracked here. Existing ignored local copies,
source URLs, and SHA-256 values remain in:

- `docs/research/pq_non_ntru_saber/papers.lock.json`
- `docs/research/ntru_saber/papers.lock.json`
